It Is All About Social

Building a beautiful website doesn't matter if you can't get anyone to visit it. This is where social media is important. Depending on what you are trying to achieve, there is a social network out there to help drive traffic to your site.

Why Social Media?

I see social media as a powerful tool that many business aren't not utilizing correctly. As of January 2014, there are 1.3 billion monthly users on Facebook worldwide. Twitter is possibly the most interactive for of social media allowing a business to connect and converse with their customers/users. Google is the go-to search engine, yet many forget to set up their G+ and Local pages. These pages will help improve search ranking and put you on the map ... literally. I have experience setting up, running accounts and building an audience across all the major networks.

How to Get Started

When setting up your social media plan, there are questions you need to ask.

  • What social networks are you currently utilizing?
  • How often are you posting?
  • What are you posting?
    • Are you posting original content from your website?
    • Are you resharing content from an external source?
  • Do you have photos or videos to post?
  • Are you posts getting interactions? (Likes, comments, shares)
  • Is there a blog on your website?
  • Is the content of that blog original content or a repost of external content?

Once you answer these questions you can start setting up how you approach.
